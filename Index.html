Creamy pasta with chicken and green pesto


Image source

About

Super quick creamy pasta with green pesto and chicken makes a great weekday meal.

Ingredients

  • 50 g butter
  • 2 spring onions, finely sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 200 g ready-roasted chicken breasts, diced
  • 50 g cooked ham, diced
  • 100 g green pesto of your choice
  • 100 g sour cream
  • 50 g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 150 ml milk
  • 250 g cooked short pasta shapes (farfalle, ziti, fusilli)

Preparation

  1. Sauté spring onions and garlic for 2-3 minutes in butter.
  2. Add ham, continue to sauté briefly.
  3. Stir in the chicken and heat through.
  4. Stir in sour cream, pesto and grated cheese.
  5. Add milk stir.
  6. Add cooked pasta, stir to coat. Serve immediately with more grated cheese.
